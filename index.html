Platform Features

Built for Gumla’s real vendor workflow

The portal gives every vendor a clean, isolated workspace to manage their slice of the marketplace.

🔗

Odoo 18 Integration

Real-time two-way sync with Odoo ERP. Prices you update here reflect instantly in the central system — no delays, no discrepancies.

🔒

Vendor Data Isolation

Each vendor can only see and manage their own products. JWT-secured sessions with role-based access ensure complete data privacy.

✏️

Inline Price Editing

Edit prices directly in the product table. Update one product or bulk-select dozens — with validation and conflict detection built in.

📦

Stock Visibility

See your current inventory levels alongside pricing. Get low-stock alerts before you run out so you can plan ahead.

📊

Change History

Every price update is logged with timestamp and user. Full audit trail keeps your operations transparent and accountable.

📱

Responsive Design

Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile. Manage your product catalog from anywhere — the office, a warehouse, or on the go.

How it works

From vendor login to synced in four steps

1

Request Access

Submit your vendor application. Our team reviews and creates your Odoo-linked account within 24 hours.

2

Log In Securely

Use your credentials to access the portal. Sessions are JWT-protected and expire automatically.

3

Manage Products

View your assigned product catalog. Add prices, edit existing ones, or bulk-update with one click.

4

Sync to Odoo

Every save triggers an API write to Odoo 18. Your prices are live across the marketplace instantly.
